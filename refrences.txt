Royal Poker CFR Project References

Core Academic Papers:
1. Johansen, M. (2013). "Measuring the Size of Large No-Limit Poker Games". 
   University of Alberta. [Describes Royal Hold'em variant]

2. Gibson, R. & Waugh, K. (2015). "Pure CFR: Solving Imperfect Information Games 
   Using Only Integer Regret Values". AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence.

3. Waugh, K. et al. (2015). "Understanding Abstraction Pathologies in 
   Extensive-Form Games". AAMAS. [Action translation theory]

4. Bowling, M. et al. (2015). "Heads-up Limit Hold'em Poker is Solved". Science.
   [Background on CFR in poker]
